About Me

My name is Michael Lawrence. I am a Internet Technologies major at Queensborough Community College. Before coming to QCC a year ago I had designed a couple of websites using Apples iWeb product, a drag and drop widget tool without access to the code.

 In the last 12 months at QCC I have been exposed to Mobile Platforms, Java Script, Java, C++, XML, PHP, MySQL, XHTML, CSS, GIT, GITHub, Networking, VPNs, media streaming, 3-D Printing, 2-D & 3-D Design, Cloud Computing and Ruby on Rails.

It has been an incredibly productive year thanks to the excellent staff, faculty and curriculum at Queensborough Community College.
