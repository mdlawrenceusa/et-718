Frequently Asked Questions

 

Q:What can be expected as an outreach objective?
A:Typically 10 % to 15% of the people in attendance make decisions at a program.  The result will depend primarily on the adoption rate and activity level several weeks leading up to the program.

Q: What are the costs involved in putting on the performance?
A: If the congregation strongly commits to using the program for evangelistic purposes the ministry is responsible for $300 for the Standard Program and $1,000 for Theatrically Enhanced Program.

Q: What is the lead time necessary to schedule a program?
A: Five to eight weeks.
